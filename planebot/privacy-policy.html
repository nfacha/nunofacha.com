PlaneTracker Discord Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023-06-28

Thank you for choosing to use the PlaneTracker Discord Bot. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect and how we use, store, and disclose this information.

By using the PlaneTracker Discord Bot, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information required to respond to your queries effectively. This information includes:

  • Your Discord username and ID
  • Your Discord server ID (if used in a server context)

We do not collect any other personal or sensitive data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information in the following ways:

  • To respond to your commands or queries
  • To provide updates or relevant information about the PlaneTracker Bot
  • To improve the functionality of the PlaneTracker Bot

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store or retain any of your information beyond the extent necessary to respond to your commands or queries. Once the PlaneTracker Bot has finished processing your request, your data is immediately discarded from our temporary storage.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose your data with any third parties.

5. Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or data processing, please contact us at planebot[at]facha.dev.

By using the PlaneTracker Bot, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
